For those that have eyes to see, let them see

At Cosmic Harmonium, we share the stories of awakenings—a journey of remembering our shared heartbeat, even as each of us awakens in our own way. Here, you’ll find not just a blog, but a gathering place for kindred souls who feel the lovingly charged hum of this present moment—and the comfort of knowing you are not alone. Whether you've glimpsed it too—the spark of presence, the echo of cosmic connection—this space is for you. Our Mission To honor and illuminate the path of individual awakening, through heartfelt stories, art, and shared resonance—so that every reader knows they are not alone in their remembering. I walk this path not alone—but with my mentors and guides—through visual expression and creative insight. Throughout the site, you'll discover artworks born from observation and insight, available for purchase to anyone who resonates with them.
